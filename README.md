# Hi, I'm Tracy Van Holt, PhD 👋

**Systems Scientist · Applied AI Researcher · Spatial & Network Analyst**

I work where knowledge graphs end — in origin communities, smallholder networks, and first-mile supply chains where fragmented data, not production capacity, is what limits decisions. I bring three capabilities that trade flow data alone cannot provide: **spatial intelligence** linking supply chain nodes to land cover and environmental systems; **consensus analysis** measuring whether actors genuinely share standards or only appear to; and **human-in-the-loop AI evaluation** catching hallucinations and failure modes before they reach decisions.

---

## 🔬 What I Do

**Human-in-the-Loop AI Evaluation**
I identify where AI models break down — hallucinations, misrepresentations, misclassifications — and design structured frameworks to catch and correct them before outputs reach decisions. Applied across NLP pipelines, ESG datasets, supply-chain analytics, and spatial systems.

**Spatial Intelligence & First-Mile Systems**
I link supply chain actors to land cover change, ocean systems, and environmental data at scales from local fishing communities to global commodity flows. I know what first-mile data looks like versus what gets reported — and where that gap creates compliance and decision risk.

**Consensus & Standards Analysis**
I use cultural consensus methods to measure whether actors genuinely share beliefs, standards, or practices — or only appear to. Applied to sustainability certification, ESG alignment, and supply chain governance. Predicts where enforcement gaps will emerge before they do.

**Applied Research Leadership**
Founded and directed an interdisciplinary research network. Led programs at NYU Stern and the Stockholm Resilience Centre. Deep familiarity with compliance frameworks: EUDR, SASB, TNFD, SEC Reg S-K, UNDP SDGs. Secured ~$1M in competitive grants across NSF, NASA, RWJ Foundation, and others.

## 🌍 Selected Work

**Supply Chain AI & Information Systems**
- Mapped 107 seafood supply chains across 50 stakeholder leaders — revealed that information asymmetry, not resource scarcity, drives coordination failure
- Analyzed Ceylon cinnamon supply chain in Sri Lanka — revealed fragmented information (not production capacity) limits market access
- Evaluated ESG outputs for Fortune 500 clients (Mars, McDonald's, Big-Box Retail, Automotive) — revealed sustainability-driven supply chain organization generates measurable financial returns

**AI Evaluation & NLP**
- Designed and benchmarked NLP pipelines for the Office of Naval Research: semi-automated methods achieved ~88% precision vs. ~38% for fully automated — quantifying the precision gap and the value of human-in-the-loop design
- Evaluated and iteratively refined AI outputs across supply-chain and analytical workflows; identified hallucinations and failure modes in production contexts
- Developed and validated ML classifications for remote sensing; diagnosed systematic model errors that propagate downstream

**Spatial & Network Modeling**
- Built multi-layer behavioral networks linking 106 fishers, 311 species, 11 communities, and 17 management areas — revealing cross-scale interactions between land, ocean, and human behavior
- Validated machine learning land classifications at ~200 km local and ~1,200 km regional scales — revealed that misclassifying tree plantations as native forest distorts environmental models

**Curriculum & AI Literacy**
- Designed AI-assisted curriculum for ASU: 5 modules, 20 skills, 4 datasets — demonstrated that non-experts can evaluate spatial AI outputs with structured frameworks
